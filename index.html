Welcome to GDSC COMSATS ATD

Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a student-run organization for anyone interested in Google developer technology. Students from any undergraduate or graduate program who want to learn more about programming, developing and other technical education are welcome to attend. Students that join a GDSC expand their expertise in a peer-to-peer learning environment while also developing solutions for local businesses and communities. The members of the GDSC get a wide knowledge about the job market of the region
